Outskirts

As the first region explored by new players, Outskirts features a tutorial area and offers many low risk opportunities to learn basic game mechanics and interact with creatures.

Return

It features many poles and pipes that allow easier evasion of predators, it's a relatively small, interconnected region, making it harder to get lost or cornered. It is devoid of the game's tougher predators, prominently featuring Green and Pink Lizards.

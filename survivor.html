Survivor

A nimble omnivore, both predator and prey. Lost in a harsh and indifferent land you must make your own way, with wit and caution as your greatest assets.

The classic Rain World experience, The Survivor must deal with the ever-present threat of predators while trying to find their way through this hostile world. Survivor deals the standard amount of damage with Spears and encounters more creature variety (for example, Dropwigs are entirely absent from Monk's campaign but appear in a select few locations as Survivor).

Opening

The introductory cutscene revolves around a family of Slugcats. They are shown resting in a nest, walking across a pole and hunting for food together. While eating Batflies, The Survivor notices a few droplets of rain. A dangerous storm breaks out, and as the group attempts to make it back to a safe place, the Survivor slips and falls into the drains below. The final scene shows the Survivor standing in a new and unknown environment, and shortly after it fades out, the game begins.

Starting Karma: 1
